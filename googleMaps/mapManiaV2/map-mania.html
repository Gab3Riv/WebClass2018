How To Play

NOTE: If The Music Isn't Playing Go To chrome://flags/#autoplay-policy And Select No User Gesture Is Required.

To win the game you must reveal all 10 locations. Locations will be selected at random.

You will know if you are getting closer to the location if the music is getting louder. The closer you are, the louder it gets, and vice versa.

As soon as your game starts the clock will start ticking which is your "Score".

Try to get the lowest score possible.

Good Luck!
